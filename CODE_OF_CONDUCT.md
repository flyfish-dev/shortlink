# Code of Conduct

AI Shortlink welcomes people who want to improve dependable, self-hosted link infrastructure. Participation in Issues, pull requests, reviews, and other project spaces is expected to remain professional and constructive.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes:

- Discussing user impact, evidence, tradeoffs, and code with clarity.
- Respecting different levels of experience, languages, identities, and viewpoints.
- Giving specific feedback and allowing others to revise their work.
- Acknowledging mistakes, correcting them, and sharing relevant context.
- Protecting private data and reporting sensitive security issues privately.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, repeated disruption, deliberate misrepresentation, or bad-faith demands.
- Publishing another person's private information or project secrets.
- Using project spaces primarily for spam, unrelated promotion, or abusive link distribution.
- Retaliation against someone who reports a concern in good faith.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository spaces and whenever someone represents the project in public or private community interactions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, code, Issues, and other contributions that violate these standards. They may issue a warning, limit participation, or permanently ban an account depending on severity and history.

For an immediate public problem, use GitHub's report or block controls and notify a maintainer without reposting harmful content. For a sensitive concern, contact a maintainer through an available private channel and keep identifying evidence out of public Issues. Security vulnerabilities must follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately where appropriate, and made without retaliation. Maintainers who are directly involved in a complaint should step back from the decision when another maintainer is available.
